SWIM Web Privacy Policy

This privacy policy has been compiled in addition to the UTEP Web Privacy Policy to inform users about how their information is being collected and used on the SWIM web-based system referred to as “SWIM” in this document.

When does SWIM collect your information?

  • When you complete the registration form for creating a new user account.
  • When you visualize outputs of the modeling tools provided by SWIM.
  • When model executions and errors are generated through your interaction with SWIM.
  • When you allow SWIM to connect to your HydroShare account.

What personal information does SWIM collect when you register on the website?

When registering on the SWIM website, you will be asked to enter the following personal information: name, last name, email address, and an access password.

How does SWIM use your information?

  • To personalize and customize your experiences as part of a recommender system.
  • Communicating new features or version updates of the system.
  • Operating, maintaining, and improving the system for you and other users.
  • To invite you to SWIM workshops, webinars, or other related events.
  • To connect with third-party collaborating platforms with your prior permission.

How does SWIM protect your information?

  • Using up-to-date security certificates to create an encrypted communication channel.
  • Data storage is user and password protected with limited direct access.
  • Performing vulnerability scans on our host servers.
  • Encrypting passwords in our databases.

Note: The above protection mechanisms may not be sufficient or 100% secure, hence absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Does SWIM use 'cookies'?

SWIM uses cookies, administered by SWIM, for storing login credentials, user authorization levels, tracking of model output rankings and HydroShare authentication.

Third-party disclosure

  • Personal data stored in SWIM does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties.
  • Tracking of interactions with SWIM may be used for academic research purposes and only aggregated, non-identifiable information will be used in publications or reports.

What rights do you have regarding your personal information?

  • Access: You have the right to request your personal information stored by SWIM and what is the purpose of its use. You may be asked for proof of identity. Requested information will be delivered only to the registered email address.
  • Rectification: You have the right to request changes on any of your personal information.
  • Erasure: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information. All your personal information will be deleted from the SWIM user database and the account will no longer be accessible, you can register again at any time after deletion.

How long does SWIM retain your personal information?

Your personal information will be retained throughout the lifetime of SWIM or when a request to delete it is made by you. When SWIM is permanently taken offline, all user accounts and personal information will be deleted from SWIM.

SWIM Terms of Use

By using SWIM you agree to be bound by these Terms. If you disagree with any part of these terms, you shall not use or register on SWIM.

User Registration

When registering on SWIM for a new user account you must be over the age of 13. Any account found to be owned by a user under the age of 13 will be terminated immediately and personal information will be deleted from the SWIM user database without prior notice.

Termination

SWIM may terminate or suspend your user account immediately, without prior notice or liability, for any reason whatsoever, including breach of SWIM Terms of Use.
